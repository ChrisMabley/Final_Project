Find your Fish:

Bodies of Water and the types of fish that live in them

Rivers

Populated mainly by smaller freshwater fish, but also used as bredding grounds for migratory salt water fish

Ponds

Populated with varieties of smaller freshwater fish, depending on the region. Being a smaller body of water limites the potential for bio-diversity

Lakes

Home to the much larger species of freshwater fish. They also have much more diverse populations due to their much larger size.

Ocean

Convering 70% of the planet and stretching to many miles deep, the variety of species far outweighs any other body of water.
